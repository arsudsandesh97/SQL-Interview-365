# SQL Scenario-Based Interview Question: Identifying At-Risk SaaS Customers with Declining Engagement

## Business Scenario
You are a Data Analyst at "TaskFlowPro", a SaaS company offering project management solutions. TaskFlowPro has several subscription tiers: 'Basic', 'Premium', and 'Enterprise'. The sales and customer success teams are concerned about customer churn, especially among higher-tier customers who might be underutilizing the product despite paying for advanced features. Your task is to proactively identify 'at-risk' customers who have an active 'Premium' or 'Enterprise' subscription but show a significant drop in their engagement with a key feature: 'Active Projects'. Specifically, they want to find users whose 'Active Projects' usage for the current month is less than 50% of their average monthly 'Active Projects' usage over the previous two *complete* months. This analysis should only include users who have maintained an active 'Premium' or 'Enterprise' subscription for at least 3 *complete* months prior to the current month to ensure a stable usage history.

Assume:
```sql
CURRENT_DATE = '2023-10-26'
```

# Task
Write a SQL query to identify 'at-risk' users. An 'at-risk' user is defined by the following criteria:
1.  They have an active 'Premium' or 'Enterprise' subscription as of `CURRENT_DATE`.
2.  They have maintained this active 'Premium' or 'Enterprise' subscription for at least 3 *complete* months prior to the current month (`CURRENT_DATE`'s month).
3.  Their total 'Active Projects' usage for the current month (`CURRENT_DATE`'s month) is less than 50% of their average monthly 'Active Projects' usage over the *previous two complete months* (i.e., August and September 2023, given `CURRENT_DATE = '2023-10-26'`).

The query should return `user_id`, `plan_name`, `current_month_usage` (total usage for `CURRENT_DATE`'s month), and `prev_2_months_avg_usage` (average usage from the two preceding complete months).

---
