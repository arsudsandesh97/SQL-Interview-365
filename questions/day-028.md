# SQL Scenario-Based Interview Question: Identifying At-Risk Customers Due to Engagement Decay

## Business Scenario
You are a Data Analyst at "TrendPulse," a growing e-commerce company specializing in fashion. The Marketing team is concerned about customer retention. They've observed that customers often churn after a period of declining engagement with the website (fewer visits, no purchases). They want to proactively identify "at-risk" customers *before* they fully churn, so targeted re-engagement campaigns can be launched. Your task is to develop a SQL query that identifies these customers based on a defined drop in activity.

Assume:
```sql
CURRENT_DATE = '2023-10-31'
```

# Task
Identify all customers who are "at-risk" due to a significant drop in engagement. A customer is considered "at-risk" if they meet ALL of the following criteria:

1.  **Established Customer:** They registered more than 90 days before `CURRENT_DATE`.
2.  **Previously Engaged:** They had at least 10 unique days of activity in the period *before* the last 30 days (i.e., from their `registration_date` up to `CURRENT_DATE - 31 days`). This ensures we're only looking at customers who were historically active.
3.  **Significant Recent Drop:** The number of unique days of activity in the *last 30 days* (`CURRENT_DATE - 30 days` to `CURRENT_DATE`) is less than 25% of their number of unique days of activity in the *preceding 60 days* (`CURRENT_DATE - 90 days` to `CURRENT_DATE - 31 days`). Also, to make this comparison valid, the customer must have had at least 1 unique day of activity in the *preceding 60 days*.

Return a list of `customer_id` for all "at-risk" customers.
