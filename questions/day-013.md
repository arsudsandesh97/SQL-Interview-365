# SQL Scenario-Based Interview Question: Identify At-Risk E-commerce Customers

## Business Scenario
You are a Data Analyst for an E-commerce company. The marketing team wants to proactively identify "at-risk" customers who have shown a significant drop in their purchasing frequency after their initial engagement with the platform. These customers will be targeted with special re-engagement campaigns to prevent churn.

The marketing team defines an "at-risk" customer using the following criteria:
1.  The customer has made at least **2 purchases in total**.
2.  Their **first purchase date + 60 days** must be less than or equal to `CURRENT_DATE`. This ensures enough time has passed to observe their subsequent purchasing behavior.
3.  Their **average monthly purchase count** in the period *after* their first 60 days since their first purchase (up to `CURRENT_DATE`) is **less than 50%** of their **average monthly purchase count** within their *first 60 days* since their first purchase.

Assume:
```sql
CURRENT_DATE = '2023-11-15'
```
