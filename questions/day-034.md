# SQL Scenario-Based Interview Question: Identifying At-Risk SaaS Premium Customers

## Business Scenario
A leading SaaS company, "InnovatePro," offers a project management tool with different subscription tiers. Their "Premium" tier includes an exclusive feature called "AI Assistant for Task Prioritization," which is a key differentiator and a significant driver of customer retention. The Head of Customer Success has observed an increasing churn rate among premium customers and suspects it's linked to a decline in engagement with this critical AI feature. As a Data Analyst, you are tasked with identifying premium customers who are at high risk of churning due to their engagement patterns with the "AI Assistant for Task Prioritization" feature. This list will be used for targeted re-engagement campaigns.

Assume:
```sql
CURRENT_DATE = '2023-10-26'
```

# Task
Identify `user_id` and `subscription_tier` for all *Premium* customers who are considered "at-risk" based on their engagement with the "AI Assistant for Task Prioritization" feature. A customer is considered "at-risk" if they meet *either* of the following conditions:

1.  **Declining Engagement (Recent Users):** The customer is a premium subscriber and has `registration_date` before April 1, 2023. They used the 'AI Assistant for Task Prioritization' feature at least once in the *most recent full month* (September 2023 for `CURRENT_DATE = '2023-10-26'`). AND their total usage count of this specific feature in the *prior 3 full months* (April, May, June 2023) was *greater than 0*. AND their total usage count of this specific feature in the *last 3 full months* (July, August, September 2023) is less than 50% of their total usage count of this specific feature in the *prior 3 full months* (April, May, June 2023).

2.  **Persistent Non-Adopter (Long-Term Premium):** The customer is a premium subscriber whose `registration_date` is before April 1, 2023 (i.e., they have been a premium customer for at least 6 full months relative to `CURRENT_DATE`). AND they have *never* used the 'AI Assistant for Task Prioritization' feature *at any point* since their registration.

Return the `user_id`, `subscription_tier`, and a `risk_reason` column indicating which condition they met ('Declining Engagement' or 'Persistent Non-Adopter'). If a user meets both conditions (though unlikely given their definitions), prioritize 'Declining Engagement' as the `risk_reason`.
