# SQL Scenario-Based Interview Question: Product Sales Anomaly Detector

## Business Scenario
You are a Data Analyst at a large e-commerce company specializing in electronics. The operations team is keen on proactively identifying products that exhibit unusual sales patterns to investigate potential underlying issues, such as unexpected stockouts, successful viral marketing, or even data entry errors in sales records. Your task is to develop an SQL query that flags products whose sales quantity in the *most recent complete week* significantly deviates (more than 50% up or down) from their *average weekly sales quantity over the preceding 8 complete weeks*. This analytical insight will help the operations team prioritize which products to investigate.

Assume:
```sql
CURRENT_DATE = '2023-10-27'
```
(Given `CURRENT_DATE = '2023-10-27'` which is a Friday, the "most recent complete week" would be Sunday, October 15, 2023 to Saturday, October 21, 2023. The "preceding 8 complete weeks" would span from Sunday, August 20, 2023 to Saturday, October 14, 2023.)

# Task
Write an SQL query to identify `product_id`, `product_name`, the `most_recent_week_sales_quantity`, and the `eight_week_avg_sales_quantity` for products where the `most_recent_week_sales_quantity` is either less than 50% of the `eight_week_avg_sales_quantity` OR more than 150% of the `eight_week_avg_sales_quantity`.

Exclude products that had zero sales in all 9 weeks (the recent week and the 8 preceding weeks).
Order the results by `product_name` in ascending order.

For the given `CURRENT_DATE = '2023-10-27'`:
- The `most_recent_full_week` is from `2023-10-15` (Sunday) to `2023-10-21` (Saturday).
- The `eight_weeks_prior` period is from `2023-08-20` (Sunday) to `2023-10-14` (Saturday).
