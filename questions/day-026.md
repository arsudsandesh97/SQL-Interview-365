# SQL Scenario-Based Interview Question: Identifying High-Return-Risk Customers and Their Problematic Products

## Business Scenario
You are a Data Analyst at "FashionNova Inc.", a rapidly growing online fashion retailer. The operations team has noticed an increasing trend in product returns, which significantly impacts profitability and inventory management. They suspect a subset of customers might be frequently returning items, potentially abusing the return policy. Your task is to identify these "high-return-risk" customers and pinpoint the top products they frequently return so the company can investigate further, potentially refine return policies, or improve product descriptions.

Assume:
```sql
CURRENT_DATE = '2023-10-26'
```

# Task
Identify "high-return-risk" customers and, for each of them, list their top 3 most frequently returned products within the last 6 months from `CURRENT_DATE`.

A customer is considered "high-return-risk" if, within this 6-month period (from `DATE_SUB(CURRENT_DATE, INTERVAL 6 MONTH)` to `CURRENT_DATE`):
1.  Their total *quantity of returned items* is strictly greater than 3.
2.  Their total *value of returned items* is strictly greater than 50% of their total *purchased item value*.

The final output should contain the customer's ID, customer name, total purchased value, total returned value, total returned items count, and a comma-separated list of their top 3 most frequently returned product names. When determining the top 3 products, order by the total `quantity_returned` for that product (descending), then by `product_id` (ascending) for ties. If a customer has fewer than 3 unique returned products, list all of them. Order the final results by `customer_id`.
