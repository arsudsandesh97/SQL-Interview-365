# SQL Scenario-Based Interview Question: Subscription Stickiness and Initial Renewal Analysis

## Business Scenario
An E-commerce company offering subscription boxes is keen on understanding the long-term loyalty and retention of its customer base. They have monthly records of active subscriptions for each customer. The Head of Operations has identified two key metrics for customer loyalty: the longest continuous period a customer maintains an active subscription, and whether customers successfully renew their *very first* subscription. This analysis will help segment customers, identify critical retention points, and inform marketing strategies.

Assume:
```sql
CURRENT_DATE = '2024-03-01'
```

# Task
Write a SQL query that returns the following information for each customer:
1.  `customer_id`: The unique identifier for the customer.
2.  `max_consecutive_months`: The longest continuous period (in months) that the customer had an active subscription. A continuous period means consecutive `subscription_month` entries without any gaps.
3.  `initial_renewal_status`: A flag ('Yes' or 'No') indicating if the customer successfully renewed their *very first* subscription. This means they had an active subscription for their first ever recorded month AND the immediate subsequent month.

Order the results by `customer_id`.
