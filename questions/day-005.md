# SQL Scenario-Based Interview Question: Monthly Subscriber Lifecycle Analytics for SaaS

## Business Scenario
You are a Data Analyst at "Subscribify," a SaaS company offering various subscription plans. The Head of Product wants to understand the monthly dynamics of their subscriber base. Specifically, they need a report for a given month that categorizes customer events into 'New Subscribers', 'Reactivated Subscribers', and 'Churned Subscribers', and then calculates the 'Net Subscriber Change'. This report is crucial for evaluating marketing campaigns, product retention strategies, and overall business health.

Assume:
```sql
CURRENT_DATE = '2023-04-01'
```

---

# Database Setup

## Create subscriptions Table
```sql
CREATE TABLE subscriptions (
    subscription_id INT PRIMARY KEY,
    customer_id INT NOT NULL,
    plan_id VARCHAR(50) NOT NULL,
    start_date DATE NOT NULL,
    cancellation_date DATE -- NULL if subscription is active or expired naturally without explicit cancellation
);
```

# Task
For the `analysis_month` starting on **'2023-03-01'**, calculate the following metrics:

1.  **New Subscribers**: Count of unique customers whose *first-ever* subscription `start_date` falls within the `analysis_month`.
2.  **Reactivated Subscribers**: Count of unique customers who started *any* subscription (`start_date`) within the `analysis_month` AND their *first-ever* subscription `start_date` was *before* the `analysis_month`.
3.  **Churned Subscribers**: Count of unique customers who have at least one subscription with a `cancellation_date` falling within the `analysis_month`, AND who do NOT have any subscription with a `start_date` falling within the `analysis_month`.
4.  **Net Subscriber Change**: Calculate (`New Subscribers` + `Reactivated Subscribers`) - `Churned Subscribers`.

Return a single row with these four metrics.
